The AD Company

The AD Company is an Advertisement firm that helps small startups to grow by providing them large reach at the least cost possible. Our aim is to reduce the number of startups that shut down due to less capital to Advertise.

The Milkman Network

India is the largest producer of milk and the largest human chain network in India is the Milkman Network. So, we decided to use this network for advertisement. We will contact centralized dairies in India and we act like a central hub for them and we will use their network for ad distribution.

You might we wondering why the dairies will do it?

  • PROFIT: WE WILL BE PAYING THEM TO DO THIS WORK
  • FREE BAGS: ALL THE BAGS WILL BE PROVIDED FREE OF COST
  • IMPROVED SERVICE: PROVIDING MILK IN ECO FRIENDLY PLASTIC BAGS WILL IMPROVE THEIR SERVICE AND THEIR CUSTOMER WILL FEEL GOOD ABOUT IT.
  • SOCIAL CAUSE: HELPING SMALL BUSINESSES AND STARTUPS TO GROW

Ads

Our ads are designed using memes, illustrations and comic strips to grab more attention of the audience.

Bags

Our ads are printed on 100% Water Soluble Bio degradable plastic bags as they are eco-friendly and available at reasonable cost at several places in India.

Target Specific Advertisement

The benefit of milkman network is that we can do target specific advertisement in Print ads and we will be the first to do this at large scale in print ads. Whenever a house subscribes to the dairy for their service, they fill a subscription form. We will ask the dairies to add few more columns in their form (as shown above) so we can get some specific information about that house and later we can use this information for target specific advertisement.

Milkman's Distribution

Now the milkman will put the milk packet or box in our bags and deliver it to the specific house.

Dairy

After the bags reach the dairy, they will be handed over to particular milkman based on the house they go to deliver milk.

We will send the details about the campaign with the bags so dairy can ensure proper target specific advertisement.

Transportation

After the Ads are printed on the bags, they will be transported to the dairy for distribution purpose.

The number of bags and type of ads to be transported to which dairy will be decided on the basis of target audience of that particular ad.

Printing

Our Ads will be printed on 100% Bio Degradable Water Soluble Eco-Friendly Bags which are manufactured by several companies in India.

Advertisement Designing

After the company is ready to be our client, we will design their advertisement using memes, illustrations, comic strips etc. Our designing team will do this.
